## Vision

NERV is the first blockchain that simultaneously achieves:

‚úÖ **Privacy by default** ‚Äì No addresses, balances, amounts, or metadata ever exposed on-chain.  
üöÄ **Infinite horizontal scalability** ‚Äì >1 million sustained TPS with no theoretical upper bound via dynamic neural sharding.  
üõ°Ô∏è **Post-quantum security from genesis** ‚Äì Full NIST-standard lattice-based cryptography (Dilithium-3, ML-KEM-768).  
üß† **Endogenous intelligence** ‚Äì Nodes are rewarded for useful-work federated learning that perpetually improves the network's own encoders and predictors.

Combined with 5-hop TEE-bound anonymous routing, AI-native optimistic consensus, and a pure useful-work economy, NERV becomes a self-improving, quantum-immune nervous system for private money and computation.

> **There is no foundation, no pre-mine, no VC allocation, no hidden treasuries. The launch is provably fair, and the protocol is fully open from day one.**

## üéØ Ethos & Principles

We are committed to:

1. **Uncompromising privacy** as the default, not an opt-in
2. **Radical openness** ‚Äì Everything auditable, no hidden logic or allocations
3. **Provable fairness** ‚Äì Zero pre-mine, transparent emissions, community governance from genesis
4. **Post-quantum readiness** ‚Äì No legacy curves in critical paths
